Fuego Dual-Asset Economy

HⲶ∆T — On-Chain Floating Supply-Rate Flatcoin

HⲶ∆T is Fuego's native floating supply-rate flatcoin, introduced in v1.10.00 "AzorAhai." It is minted on-chain by one-way, irreversible burning of base-layer XFG collateral and stabilized by a deterministic Proportional-Integral (PI) controller that factors in Consumer Price Index data and fiat inflation rates alongside Hearth AMM market conditions. HⲶ∆T exists as its own L1 asset (assetId=0x01) with a fully isolated ring signature decoy pool for zero-knowledge privacy.

Unlike a stablecoin pegged to a fixed fiat value, HⲶ∆T targets purchasing power preservation — its floating supply rate adjusts to offset CPI and inflation trends, maintaining real-world value over time through algorithmic monetary policy.

Burn2Mint Economics

HⲶ∆T is created exclusively through one-way burning of XFG. The mint formula is:

HⲶ∆Tminted = XFGconsumed / RedemptionPrice

  • One-way conversion only — XFG → HⲶ∆T is irreversible at consensus level. No reverse minting, no inflationary exploit.
  • Dynamic RedemptionPrice — Not a static scalar. Updated automatically at 900-block epoch boundaries by the PI controller incorporating CPI and fiat inflation data.
  • Burned XFG recycles — Destroyed collateral enters the EternalFlame (BankingIndex::addForeverDeposit), organically sustaining future block rewards.
  • Privacy-preserving — HⲶ∆T outputs pull decoys exclusively from the HⲶ∆T output tree, eliminating cross-asset contamination in ring signatures.

Hearth AMM — On-Chain Liquidity

The Hearth constant-product automated market maker runs natively inside block verification (Rxfg × RhⲶ∆t = K) with a flat 1% (100 bps) swap fee. All fees are distributed pro-rata to Liquidity Providers (LPs) who deposit HⲶ∆T/XFG pairs into the pool. No external routers, no web3 dependencies.

Q64.64 PI Controller — Deterministic Monetary Policy

The PI controller extracts pricing signals from Hearth's own Time-Weighted Average Price (TWAP) across each 900-block epoch, combined with CPI and fiat inflation reference data. Uses pure fixed-point integer arithmetic (Q64.64) for deterministic consensus across all CPU architectures.

  • Purchasing power below target → PI increases the spend rate, increasing HⲶ∆T supply to restore value alignment.
  • Purchasing power above target → PI reduces spend rate, constricting supply growth.
  • CPI + inflation data — Incorporated into the RedemptionPrice calculation so the flatcoin tracks real-world purchasing power, not a fixed dollar peg.
  • No oracles, no governance votes, no multisigs — pure deterministic math at consensus level.

Commitment Deposits (CDs)

Commitment Deposits are time-locked deposits denominated entirely in HⲶ∆T — both principal and interest are held and settled in HⲶ∆T. Yields are paid from atomic swap fees (SwapXFG) generated by cross-chain swap activity, not from Hearth AMM trading fees.

Key Features

  • Principal + interest in HⲶ∆T — deposit and yield are both denominated in the flatcoin
  • Yield from SwapXFG — atomic swap fee revenue flows to CD holders at each epoch
  • Non-inflationary — yield sourced from network activity fees, not supply expansion
  • Privacy flywheel — more locked deposits = larger ring signature pool = stronger privacy for all
  • 900-block epoch — yield distributes at each epoch boundary

How It Works

  1. User locks HⲶ∆T into a Commitment Deposit for a chosen term
  2. The deposit is recorded on-chain
  3. Each epoch (900 blocks), SwapXFG atomic swap fees are harvested and distributed pro-rata to CD holders
  4. Yield is settled in HⲶ∆T
  5. At term expiry, principal (HⲶ∆T) is returned to the user

The Dual Flywheel

  1. Users want purchasing-power-preserving money → demand for HⲶ∆T → burn XFG → mint HⲶ∆T
  2. HⲶ∆T circulation drives cross-chain swap activity → SwapXFG fees accumulate
  3. SwapXFG fees → CD holders as yield → more users lock HⲶ∆T as CDs
  4. More locked HⲶ∆T → stronger privacy → more demand → back to step 1
